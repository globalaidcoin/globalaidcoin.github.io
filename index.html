In this video we have used a virtual currency called "SOSTaler".
The SOSTaler is one specific example of an AidCoin, a virtual
currency used in the humanitarian world. The name is the
fusion of the two terms "SOS" (Save Our Souls) and "Taler"
(pronounced T-ah-ler), old german for coin.
